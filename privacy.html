Tabbi Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tabbi ("Tabbi", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Tabbi mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: username and password when you create an account and sign in.
  • Profile information: information you choose to add, such as your name.
  • People and relationship data: names and details you add about people you track in Tabbi.
  • Session content: notes, transcripts, summaries, and other text you enter or generate in the app.
  • Voice input data: audio recordings you submit for transcription.
  • Derived data: extracted facts, reminders, date-related information, and calendar events generated from your session content.
  • Technical/request data: basic server logs such as IP address, request path, and timestamps.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Tabbi features.
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts.
  • Process voice interactions and produce transcripts.
  • Generate summaries, reminders, and question-answering responses.
  • Organize your people, sessions, and calendar-related information.
  • Respond to support and privacy requests.
  • Monitor reliability and troubleshoot service issues.

3. Voice, Microphone, and Speech Data

Tabbi requests microphone and speech-related permissions so you can record voice interactions and use speech functionality. If you deny microphone access, voice recording features may not work.

Audio submitted for transcription is transmitted to our backend for processing and may be sent to our AI service provider for speech-to-text. Temporary audio files are intended to be deleted after transcription processing is completed.

4. AI Processing and Third-Party Service Providers

We use third-party providers to operate core features:

  • OpenAI: used for transcription and language features (for example, generating summaries and answering questions). Content you submit (such as audio and text) may be processed by OpenAI to provide these features.
  • Database hosting (PostgreSQL/Supabase): used to store account and app data.
  • Optional vector search infrastructure: when enabled, selected text chunks and metadata may be stored to support semantic search and retrieval quality.

We share only the data reasonably needed for these providers to perform services on our behalf.

5. Legal Bases (Where Applicable)

Depending on your location, we rely on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract (to provide the Service you request).
  • Legitimate interests (to secure and improve the Service).
  • Consent (for permissions such as microphone access, where required).
  • Compliance with legal obligations.

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You can delete individual people entries in the app; this removes associated related records according to our database design.

If you want broader deletion assistance, contact us at davidsprogramplayground@gmail.com.

7. Security

We use safeguards designed to protect your information, including password hashing, token-based authentication, encrypted transport (HTTPS in production), and secure token storage on device. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Choices and Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export personal data.

To submit a privacy request, contact davidsprogramplayground@gmail.com and include enough details for us to verify and process your request.

9. Children's Privacy

Tabbi is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

10. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own by us and our service providers. Where required, we take steps intended to provide appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here and revise the "Last updated" date.

12. Contact Us

For support or privacy questions, contact: davidsprogramplayground@gmail.com
